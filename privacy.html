Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Asiatek AI, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • API Usage Data: Model requests, token consumption, and usage patterns for billing and service improvement.
  • Payment Information: Transaction records processed through our payment provider (Creem). We do not store your credit card details.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our API services
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To communicate about your account, billing, and service updates
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To prevent fraud and ensure platform security

3. Data Storage and Security

Our infrastructure is hosted on Alibaba Cloud ECS in Singapore, ensuring compliance with regional data protection regulations including PDPA. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Payment Processor (Creem): For processing subscriptions and payments
  • Upstream API Providers: API requests are forwarded to model providers (Alibaba Cloud, DeepSeek) to fulfill your requests — no personal account data is shared
  • Service Providers: Hosting and infrastructure providers as necessary to operate the service

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. API usage logs are retained for 90 days for billing dispute resolution. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting support@asiatekai.com.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We use analytics cookies to understand how our services are used. You may disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict data processing
  • Data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a notice on our website.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: support@asiatekai.com
